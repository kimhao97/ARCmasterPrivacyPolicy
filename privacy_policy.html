Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-03

ARCmaster (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a professional architectural and engineering calculation application. This Privacy Policy explains what information ARCmaster collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

ARCmaster uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous technical data, including:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App version and performance metrics
  • Anonymous usage statistics (feature usage, session duration)
  • Crash logs and error reports

All collected data is anonymized and cannot identify you personally.

1.2 Locally Stored Data

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Your calculations and engineering project data
  • Generated files (PDF, CAD, images)
  • App settings and preferences

1.3 Data We Do Not Collect

ARCmaster does not collect or request:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Location or GPS data
  • Photos, camera, microphone, or media access
  • Contacts or device files
  • Financial or payment information
  • Content of your calculations or project data

2. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Identify crashes and technical issues
  • Apply configuration updates using Firebase Remote Config
  • Ensure app stability and security

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Anonymous analytics data may be processed by:

  • Google Firebase – Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config

Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

We apply the following security measures:

  • All project data is stored locally on your device
  • Analytics data is anonymized
  • Communication with Firebase uses encrypted HTTPS/TLS

No cloud backend or remote server receives your project data.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data: Kept until you delete files or uninstall the app
  • Analytics data: Stored according to Firebase retention policy

6. Your Rights

Depending on your region (EU, US, etc.), you may have the right to:

  • Access anonymous analytics data
  • Request deletion of analytics data via Google’s privacy tools
  • Disable analytics via device settings

Because ARCmaster does not collect personal data, most data rights requests do not apply.

7. Children's Privacy

ARCmaster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available at this page.
